About the Egyptian Pantheon

The Egyptian pantheon is filled with deities tied to nature, death, and cosmic order, deeply integrated into their rituals and beliefs.

Notable Deities

  • Ra – Sun god, ruler of creation
  • Osiris – God of the afterlife
  • Isis – Goddess of magic and motherhood
  • Anubis – God of mummification
  • Horus – God of the sky and kingship
  • Set – God of chaos and storms
