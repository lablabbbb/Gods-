About the Celtic Pantheon

The Celtic pantheon is diverse, with deities tied to nature, warfare, poetry, and magic, revered across ancient Ireland, Wales, and Britain.

Notable Deities

  • The Dagda – Good god of abundance and strength
  • Brigid – Goddess of poetry and healing
  • Lugh – God of skill and crafts
  • Morrigan – Goddess of war and fate
  • Cernunnos – Horned god of wild places
  • Nuada – King of the Tuatha Dé Danann
