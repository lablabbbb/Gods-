About the Hindu Pantheon

The Hindu pantheon contains many gods and goddesses, each embodying different aspects of life, cosmic forces, and dharma (duty).

Notable Deities

  • Brahma – Creator god
  • Vishnu – Preserver of the universe
  • Shiva – Destroyer and transformer
  • Lakshmi – Goddess of wealth
  • Saraswati – Goddess of knowledge
  • Durga – Warrior goddess
