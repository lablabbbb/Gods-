About the Japanese Pantheon

The Japanese pantheon, centered on Shinto beliefs, includes kami (spirits or gods) associated with natural forces, fertility, and creation.

Notable Deities

  • Amaterasu – Sun goddess and ancestor of emperors
  • Susanoo – Storm god
  • Tsukuyomi – Moon god
  • Inari – Deity of rice and prosperity
  • Hachiman – God of war
  • Ebisu – God of fishermen and luck
