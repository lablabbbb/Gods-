About the Aztec Pantheon

The Aztec pantheon features powerful gods tied to war, agriculture, and sacrifice, reflecting their vibrant yet brutal cosmology.

Notable Deities

  • Huitzilopochtli – God of war and sun
  • Quetzalcoatl – Feathered serpent god of wisdom
  • Tlaloc – God of rain
  • Tezcatlipoca – God of night and sorcery
  • Coatlicue – Earth goddess
  • Mictecacihuatl – Lady of the dead
