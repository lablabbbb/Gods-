About the Norse Pantheon

The Norse pantheon consists of the Aesir and Vanir gods, known for tales of heroism, prophecy, and Ragnarok—the end of the world.

Notable Deities

  • Odin – All-father, god of wisdom and war
  • Thor – God of thunder and strength
  • Loki – Trickster god of mischief
  • Freya – Goddess of love and fertility
  • Tyr – God of law and heroic glory
  • Hel – Goddess of the underworld
